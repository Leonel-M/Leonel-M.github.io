Featured Projects

Superstore Sales Dashboard

I developed an interactive dashboard using Dash (Plotly + Flask) to explore sales data from a fictional superstore. I implemented dynamic filters for shipping mode, customer segment, state, month, and day of week, and generated histograms, bar charts, pie charts, and maps by processing the dataset with Pandas. This modular design ensures maintainability and scalability.

Order & Inventory Management App

I developed a custom Streamlit web application to help a small Venezuelan design and production company manage orders and inventory. The app allows client and product registration, multi-currency material tracking, and real-time dashboards for pending and overdue orders. It also generates 90-day and monthly reports to support decision-making. The project was deployed in a low-connectivity environment with local Dropbox sync for offline access.
